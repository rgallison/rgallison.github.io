Minions Love Bananas Gaming Site

An cross-branded web app that allowed users to sign up, play games, and earn prizes.
Company: Chiquita

About This Project

Chiquita teamed up with Universal to create the Minions Love Bananas campaign, which consisted of a gaming site that allowed users to sign up and play games to win rewards and earn prizes. As Lead Developer, I led a team of 4 to develop the website and games using Wordpress as a framework and integrating FunMobilityâ€™s gaming Nanosites into the site, as well as building a contest prise engine to award prizes. We also built new gaming experiences for the website, including a sticker scanner that allowed users to hunt and scan Chiquita banana stickers found on the fruit that featured the minions, a minion sticker tournament that allowed users to vote for their favorite stickers, and a minions memory game, where users raced against time to match the tiles.
